Our Story

About Us

THE HAIR ENGINEER was founded with a single passion — to create a space where luxury meets craft. We believe every visit should feel like a retreat, and every service should feel personal.

Located in the heart of Kothrud, Pune, our boutique studio brings together the finest techniques in hair care, styling, and beauty — delivered by a team of dedicated professionals who treat your hair as their art.

From precision haircuts and balayage to bridal packages and spa treatments, we offer an experience curated for discerning clients who expect nothing but the best.
